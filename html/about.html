About Us

We all want to create something great. Whether that's a hack or a vaccine, a novel or a Billboard hit, the drive to make, and make well, is a powerful force. We get it. In addition to being coders, our develops are musicians, writers, teachers, and engineers. We want to help give you and your art all the tools to succeed. Bandmate is a platform to help musicians find each other and work together. There are enough instrumentalists to go around, and we're here to help you find them. Whether you want a bassist in your town to jam with, or a flutist half the world away who also enjoys electro-ska-swing, odds are there are other people who like the same music as you and want to make it.
We'll leave the ideas to you. We're just your helpful groupies.
So what are you waiting for?
